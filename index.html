Our Story

More Than a Restaurant. An Experience.

OPIUM was born from a singular passion — the belief that great food, great drinks, and great people deserve a space worthy of the moment. Nestled in the heart of Soshanguve, we've created something the community deserves: a venue where every visit feels like a special occasion.

From sizzling grills and crafted cocktails to live music and vibrant social evenings — OPIUM is where Soshanguve comes alive. This is not simply a place to eat — it is a place to feel.
